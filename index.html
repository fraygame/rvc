Oseyo – Korean Supermarket

73-73 Charing Cross Road, London WC2H 0NE, UK How to get here

Key information

Address
73-73 Charing Cross Road, London WC2H 0NE, UK

Telephone
020 39739701

Website
http://www.oseyo.co.uk/

Opening Hours
Monday to Sunday, 10am - 8pm

See all shops See all shops

Oseyo brings a little slice of Korea to the heart of London on Charing Cross Road. Whilst it’s the perfect place to stock up on all your Korean culinary essentials, that’s not all you’ll find here.

Alongside kimchee, noodles, and mochi, you’ll find talking rice cookers, K-pop, stationery, accessories and K-beauty favourites.

If you’re in need of a pick-me-up, grab a hot steamed bun to go, with pork, red bean and vegetarian fillings to choose from.
